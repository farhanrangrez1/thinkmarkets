What is Traders’ Gym?

Traders Gym’ is a tool exclusive to Prime Wave’ traders that allows them to test their trading strategies on historical data in just a matter of minutes. This unique and innovative tool offers a simulated market environment where you can discover how your strategies would have worked in the past.

Historical market data*
Visualise your strategies against the authentic price movements of global markets, gaining invaluable insights into their potential performance.

Customisable settings
Personalise your workspaces, simulations, time frames, and instruments to align seamlessly with your preferred trading style.

Technical analysis tools*
Set up your favourite indicators, charts, and drawing tools as if you are trading in real time. Then save your analysis and apply it on your live trades.
